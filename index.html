Pizza


Pizza, an Italian dish, is a flatbread topped with various ingredients like tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings, baked at high temperatures, and is a globally popular food.

Burger


A burger, also known as a hamburger, is a dish consisting of a patty of ground meat (typically beef) placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll, often with various toppings.

Fries


French fries are a popular side dish or snack made from deep-fried, batonnet or julienne-cut potatoes, often served hot with salt and various sauces.
